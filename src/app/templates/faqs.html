I didn't get any email to confirm my request, what now?
In the homepage (home) you can find the different steps in a request process. The "Student 1" of the request form will receive email first, only after this first student confirms the request (clicking in the link sent in the email), the second student will receive his or her confirmation mail. If this conditions don't apply to you and your colleague (for example, the first student didn't receive their email after successfully filling for a request) you should contact us via email. More details can be found in contacts.
